How to Start

A simple four-step path.

This gives families, guardians, case managers, placement coordinators, and discharge planners a clear way to move forward.

1

Submit inquiry

Tell us who you are helping, the care needs, timeline, and best way to contact you.

2

Discuss fit

We talk through supervision, mobility, personal care, dementia or developmental disability needs.

3

Schedule a visit

When appropriate, families or representatives can tour and ask detailed questions.

4

Plan next steps

If the home is a good fit, we discuss documents, assessment needs, and move-in timing.

Information that helps us understand fit

  • Resident age and current city
  • Current living situation
  • Primary care and supervision needs
  • Mobility and transfer needs
  • Dementia or developmental disability support needs
  • Medication support needs
  • Behavioral or safety concerns
  • Desired move-in timeline

Referral partners welcome

We welcome inquiries from families, guardians, case managers, hospital discharge planners, placement coordinators, representatives, and community referral partners.

This page should make the process feel organized and safe, rather than leaving people unsure what to do next.
