About Us

A caring residential home where residents are known personally.

ACaring Adult Home is located in Federal Way, Washington. We provide residential care in a smaller, family-style setting for adults who need daily support, supervision, and personal care.

Our Care Philosophy

We believe good care starts with knowing each resident as a person. Every resident has their own history, preferences, strengths, routines, and care needs.

Our approach is steady, respectful, and practical. We focus on dignity, safety, consistency, and daily support that helps each resident feel seen and cared for.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, caring, and supportive home where adults who need daily assistance can live with dignity, stability, and respect.

  • Compassionate daily care
  • Respectful communication
  • Clean and comfortable living spaces
  • Supportive routines and supervision

Our Values

Care that feels personal, steady, and respectful.

  • Compassion: We treat residents with patience and kindness.
  • Safety: We focus on daily well-being and supervision.
  • Dignity: We respect each person’s privacy and preferences.
  • Consistency: We support reliable routines and care plans.
  • Connection: We encourage family involvement and meaningful engagement.
